What is Tambola / Housie?

Tambola and Housie are two names for the same popular Indian board game based on Bingo. Players receive tickets with a 3×9 grid containing 15 random numbers between 1 and 90. A host calls numbers one by one, and players mark them on their tickets. The first player to complete a winning pattern (like Top Line, Full House, or Early Five) wins the prize.

What is the Difference Between Tambola and Housie?

Tambola and Housie are the same game. "Tambola" is more commonly used in North India, while "Housie" is popular in South India, Mumbai, and among Anglo-Indian communities. The game is also related to British Bingo or Housey-Housey. Tambola Master supports both names and works for all variations of this classic board game.
